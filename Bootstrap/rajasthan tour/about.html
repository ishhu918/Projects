About us

Rajasthan-Holidays.net is a result of dedicated services provided by Memorable India Travels since 2005. As a result of persistent demand for Rajasthan tour packages and Golden Triangle tour services from both domestic and international clients we decided to launch a dedicated website for this segment.

Memorable India, has been serving clients with enthusiasm since 2005. But we were formally registered as a travel company only in 2010. We are based at New Delhi, India and we have associate offices and support staff like guides, ground operators and travel consultants in almost every major travel destination in India.

We also have business partners and sales representatives in Russia, Kazakhstan. For more information about business development and sales representative appointments please contact us.

Our trained and native travel executives have extensive knowledge about Indian destinations, weather, monuments and rules. This enables them to provide you the right itinerary, make a correct costing, recommend best hotels, protect you from hassles etc.

Rajasthan Holidays offers you customized packages, tailored to your preferences and budget. We strive to make your holiday enjoyable and you experience of Rajasthan and India unique with hand-picked spots and efficient, guides speaking your language and much more.

We match our travel arrangements with 24x7 phone support and ground support though our agents and guides at every location in the trip.
