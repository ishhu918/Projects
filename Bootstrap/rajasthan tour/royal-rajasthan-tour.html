Royal Rajasthan Tour

  1. Home
  2. Royal Rajasthan Tour

Tour Description:

Rajasthan has always been the centre of attraction for tourists round the globe. The state is rich in its diversified cultural heritage. We at Rajasthan Holidays are offering our customers best holiday package for exploring the city in every way. Rajasthan is a place whose grandeur can never be seen or explained but is experienced by own self. We offer a 16 days tour package which includes complete sight-seeing and accommodation.

Duration

15 Nights / 16 Days

Destination Covered

Delhi ➤ Neemrana ➤ Jaipur ➤ Luni ➤ Udaipur ➤ Kumbhalgarh ➤ Deogarh ➤ Kota ➤ Sawai Madhopur ➤ Karauli ➤ Agra

Day 1 ➤ Arrival in Delhi
Our representative will be there for you at Delhi International Airport and will drop you at your hotel. You can rest for a while or fix a visit to Akshardhaam Temple in Delhi. Akshardhaam Temple is brilliantly constructed and features great carvings over it. It will be a kick start to your journey in Delhi.
Day 2 ➤ Tour of Delhi
Early in the morning we will start your tour with Delhi sight-seeing. Delhi has always been the city with great historical background. Every Ruler and Emperor has attempted to leave his impression over Delhi with great structures of his era. Our tour will commence from Rajghat and will cover places like Humayun’s Tomb, Jama Masjid, Lotus Temple, Qutub Minar, India Gate and Red Fort.
Day 3 ➤ Delhi-Neemrana
After having breakfast at our Delhi hotel we will head to Neemrana, our first destination at Rajasthan. We will check-in at Neemrana Fort-Palace Heritage Hotel. This fort was once the residence of Royal family later turned into a heritage hotel. After resting for a while, we will visit Kesroli, Buddhist Vihara and statue of Hanuman, which is associated with the greatest Indian epic ‘Mahabharata’.
Day 4 ➤ Neemrana-Jaipur & Evening tour to Chokhi Dhani
We will head to the Pink city, Jaipur early in the morning. We will visit a colourful place on the outskirts of Jaipur named ‘Chokhi Dhani’. It is a resort like structure with entertainment features like Rajasthani Folk music, Folk Dance with other rejuvenating activities. Later in the night we will get back to our hotel.
Day 5 ➤ Jaipur Sightseeing Tour
We will devote our complete day to Jaipur sight-seeing. Ajmer Fort, Jantar Mantar, City Palace and Museum, Jaigarh Fort and Hawa Mahal will be our highlights including several other places.
Day 6 ➤ Jaipur-Luni
After our day long sight-seeing at Jaipur, we will head to Luni. Luni is the home of fort Chanwa. The fort is located in a deserted part of Rajasthan. The Palace Chanwa is rich in architecture and offers a great atmosphere for rejuvenation. The hospitality and service at hotel adds charm to the Palace.
Day 7 ➤ Luni–Ranakpur-Udaipur
After a great rejuvenating experience at Palace Chanwa, we will head to Udaipur with a short-time stoppage at Ranakpur. Ranakpur is famous for its Jain temples. After reaching Udaipur, we will rest for a while and start our tour with boat riding at Lake Pichola. Udaipur is famous for its lakes and landscapes. We will spend the night at Udaipur only.
Day 8 ➤ Udaipur
City Palace, Fateh Sagar Lake, Jagdish Temple, Monsoon Palace, Shilpgram will be our hotspots for the day at Udaipur.
Day 9 ➤ Udaipur-Kumbhalgarh
Kumbhalgarh fortress and Wildlife Sanctuary is famous for horse and jeep safaris. The sanctuary is spread over an area of 500 sq. Km and is a home to many dangerous wildlife species. We suggest you not forget your cameras, who knows which animal is about to cross your way. After the tiring, all-day safari we will spend the night at Kumbhalgarh hotel.
Day 10 ➤ Kumbhalgarh-Deogarh
We will leave for Deogarh after having a heavy breakfast in Kumbhalgarh. We will start our tour in Deogarh from Deogarh Palace. After Deogarh Palace, we will visit various tourist attractions of Deogarh including Dashavtar Temple, Manastamba and will provide you an opportunity to buy famous Chanderi sarees.
Day 11 ➤ Deogarh-Kota
Next on our list is Kota. After resting for a while in our hotels, we will head to local sight-seeing. Umaidbhawan will be our highlight for Kota tour. The bhawan was built in 1904 by Rajputana emperors. Boat ride at Kisore Lake, the Chambal gardens and the Kota fort are other main attractions of the trip.
Day 12 ➤ Kota-Sawai Madhopur
After having breakfast at Kota we will head to our next destination, Sawai Madhopor. Sawai Madhopor is famous for safari trips. These safari trips are known for guavas, chillies and mustard. One can take evening safari as we will not rush for anything in Sawai Madhopur.
Day 13 ➤ Safari tours in Ranthambore National Park
We will spend our day at Ranthambore National Park only. Ranthambore National Park is a home to many wild species including Tiger. We will take you for a morning and a night safari to encounter most of the animals. One can also visit the Ranthambore temple situated on a hill at Ranthambore National Park.
Day 14 ➤ Sawai Madhopur-Karauli
After having our breakfast, we will head to Karauli. Maharaja’s museum and City Palace are prime attractions of the city. Karauli is also famous for its Bazaars, which offer you great handicraft items. One can choose from wide range of handicrafts as token of love for friends and family.
Day 15 ➤ Karauli-Agra
Next on our list is visiting one of the seven wonders of the world- The Taj Mahal. A trip to India can never get complete without visiting Taj Mahal. We are ending our trip in India with the visit to Agra. Agra is also known for its Mosques and Agra fort. Agra is a heritage in itself. We will spend the night in Agra only.
Day 16 ➤ Agra-Delhi & Departure
Witnessing the rise of Sun over the magnificent Taj Mahal is a life time experience in itself. Later, we will return to our hotel and pack our bags early in the morning to check out. After having breakfast at our hotel we will head towards our start point, Delhi for flying back home or commencing a new journey.
