Colorful Rajasthan Tour

  1. Home
  2. Colorful Rajasthan Tour

Tour Description:

India is a country where one can get to see an array of different cultures and people of different religions living united. Each state of the country is well-known for its own specialty. The state of Rajasthan is popularly called as one of the most colorful states across India. Rajasthan is place bestowed with rich flora and fauna, majestic forts, scintillating monuments, beautiful havelis, picturesque lakes, handicrafts, art and architecture and most importantly an age old culture that makes the state unique from the rest of the country. The beautiful deserts of Rajasthan, especially the Thar Desert, draw the attention of many tourists all over the world. We are presenting a 15 day tour to this colorful state to explore its beauty and charm with a fun-filled experience.

Duration

14 Nights / 15 Days

Destination Covered

Delhi ➤ Jaipur ➤ Ajmer ➤ Pushkar ➤ Udaipur ➤ Jodhpur ➤ Jaisalmer ➤ Gajner ➤ Bikaner ➤ Samode ➤ Mandawa ➤ Bharatpur ➤ Agra ➤ Delhi

Day 1 ➤ Arrival in Delhi
On arriving at the Delhi airport, our representatives will take you to the hotel and help with the transfer and check-in procedures. Those who land up early in Delhi, we will arrange a guide tour for them to the nearer Aksardham temple, one of the finest temples in the country. The temple is carved with beautiful architecture and considered as a major tourist destination. We will get back to the hotel at night.
Day 2 ➤ Sightseeing tour of Delhi
After a delicious breakfast, we will leave the hotel to explore the capital city Delhi. The city has been evolved through time from a glorious past to the cosmopolitan culture of the present time. The major sightseeing of Delhi include Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple, Red Fort, India Gate etc. do not forget to visit Chandi Chowk, known to be the heart of the city. You can shop at the local shopping markets and taste the delicious sweets at Chandi Chowk. The night will be spent at the hotel.
Day 3 ➤ Delhi- Jaipur
Early morning, we will start moving towards Jaipur city. Called as the pink city, it is the capital city of the state of Rajasthan. We will relax at the hotel for sometime before leaving for Choki Dhani. The Choki Dhani village is a themed luxury resort which is mostly favored by the tourists visiting here. Staying at the hotel, you can explore the color of Rajasthan in a nutshell. The whole experience will make you feel like the trailer of a movie and will remain unforgettable. We retire in the hotel room at night.
Day 4 ➤ Sightseeing Tour of Jaipur
After the morning breakfast, we will proceed to the major sightseeing of Jaipur. One of the highlights of the Jaipur city is the amber fort. We will take an elephant ride to reach to the top of the hill. The other tourist destinations will also be explored including Jaigarh Fort, City Palace and Museum, Hawa Mahal etc. you can shop different types of home décor items, puppets, and handmade ornaments from the local markets of Jaipur before returning back to the hotel.
Day 5 ➤ Jaipur-Ajmer-Pushkar
The next day we will visit Ajmer, which is known to be the major pilgrim destination of Rajasthan. Hundreds of thousands of people from all over the country and outside, irrespective of their caste, religion and creed, visit the Ajmer darga to seek blessings and grace of Khwaja Moinuddin Chisti. We will visit the holy place and seek his blessings. We will travel to Pushkar where we will visit the Brahma temple. The temple is dedicated to Lord Brahma who, as per the Hindu mythology, is the creator of the whole universe. There are as many as 400 temples and 52 Ghats found in Pushkar. We will also make a trip to the Apteshwar temple, Rangji and Mahadev temple and Savitri temple. The night will be spent at the hotel.
Day 6 ➤ Pushkar-Udaipur
We will take an early morning visit to the Ghats of Pushkar. You will have the opportunity to see many devoted pilgrims taking a holy dip at the Pushkar lake with the temple bells ringing in the background. The view of the rising sun makes the atmosphere even more divine. We will return to the hotel and have our morning breakfast before heading for Udaipur. The Udaipur city is also called the city of lakes for its picturesque lakes that are truly mesmerizing. The beautiful palaces of the city are the other tourist attractions. After the lunch at the hotel, we will take you on a boat ride at Lake Pichola in the afternoon.
Day 7 ➤ Udaipur
Famously known as the city of lakes, the Fateh Sagar Lake attracts large number of tourists. The city palace and monsoon palace are some finest palaces among others in Udaipur. The palaces symbolize the rajput art and architecture in its carvings. We will visit to the Bharatiya Lok Kala Mandal to have a glimpse of the crafts for which Udaipur is well-known. Visiting the many places of Udaipur will bring you an essence of architecture, nature and art and crafts of Rajasthan.
Day 8 ➤ Udaipur- Jodhpur
The next day, after a five hours drive, we will reach at Jodhpur. Jodhpur is also known as the sun city of India and is filled with many tourist destinations. The must-see tourist places are Mehrangarh Fort, Umaid Bhavan Palace, Jaswant Thada etc. during our visit, what comes to the notice that most of the houses of Jodhpur are painted with blue color. We retire at the hotel room at night.
Day 9 ➤ Jodhpur-Jaisalmer
We will leave for Jaisalmer the next day for a whole new experience. Most of the structures in Jaisalmer are made with sandstone which gives an ochre yellow shade to the whole city. We will take you on a guided tour to Nathmalji ki Haveli, Manak Chowk, Salim Singhji ki Haveli, Jaisalmer Fort etc. located in the midst of the desert, the Jaisalmer city has numerous havelis and temples which are worth visiting. We will return to the hotel at night.
Day 10 ➤ Camel Safari tour in Jaisalmer
On day 10, we will take a day out and visit the Jaisalmer city. First we will head to Khuri village. We will arrange a camel safari which is the perfect way to explore the beauty of the desert and the sand dunes. We will return to the camps at Khuri village for an entertaining evening with folk dances and music programs. After the dinner, we will spend the night at the tents set up for tourists.
Day 11 ➤ Jaisalmer-Gajner
We will return to our hotel on the next morning. After breakfast, we will leave for Gajner and settle at the Gajner heritage hotel. We will arrange a jeep safari for the Gajner wildlife sanctuary which is home to numerous wild life species such as desert foxes, boar, deer and antelopes like black bucks and blue bulls. Gajner was once a hunted ground by the royalty of Rajasthan. A lake adjacent to the sanctuary is worth visiting. The wild animals also depend on the lake as it is a source of water. We will return back to the heritage hotel at night.
Day 12 ➤ Gajner-Bikaner
We will leave Gajner only to proceed towards Bikaner the next morning. Bikaner is located few kms away from Gajner. Exclusively known for its sweets and snacks, the Bikaner city has a lot in store for its tourists. The prime attractions of the city are Lallgarh Palace, Junagarh Fort and a number of beautiful temples. The camel breeding farm of Bikaner tops the list among all tourist destinations and should never be missed. We will come back to the hotel for a night’s rest.
Day 13 ➤ Bikaner-Mandawa
On the next day, we will start morning towards Mandawa, a place where most of the Marwaris and shrewd businessmen reside. Do not forget to visit the vibrant havelis of Mandawa. The walls of the havelis are painted with frescos. Once a land of rich merchants, Mandawa is often called an ‘Open Art Gallery’. It is also a ground for major Indian army recruitments.
Day 14 ➤ Mandawa-Bharatpur-Agra
We will head for Agra the following day. On the way, we will visit the Bharatpur bird sanctuary and have a view of some rare bird species of the country. After reaching Agra, our representatives will help you with the check-in process. We then visit to the beautiful Taj Mahal, known as one of the Seven Wonders of the World. The Agra fort shall not be missed as it is a world heritage site declared by the UNESCO. You can shop at the market place of Agra on the final day. We will return to the hotel in the evening.
Day 15 ➤ Agra- Delhi & Departure
An early morning visit to the Taj Mahal will be a perfect way to bring an end to this beautiful journey. After a night’s sleep we will leave for Taj Mahal early in the morning and catch its different hues. We will take you to the Delhi airport for your flight back home or arrange a flight from Agra connecting to Delhi.
