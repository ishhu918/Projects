Golden Triangle with Khajuraho Tour

  1. Home
  2. Golden Triangle with Khajuraho Tour

Tour Description:

We take you to the Khajuraho tour which is well-known for its historical significance and ancient architecture. Our journey will take off from the capital city of India to the group of monuments in Khajuraho, One of the most popular tourist destinations in the country. It has been declared as the World Heritage Site by UNESCO and has the largest group of medieval Hindu and Jain temples. We will also visit the capital city of the state of Rajasthan, the Jaipur city bestowed with its royal cultural heritage and the Agra city, a place that holds the pride of housing one of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal.

Duration

10 Nights / 11 Days

Destination Covered

Delhi ➤ Jaipur ➤ Agra ➤ Khajuraho ➤ Delhi

Day 1 ➤ Delhi
After reaching in Delhi, you will be escorted to the hotel by our representative. After the transfer and check-in process, we will relax at the hotel room for the rest of the day.
Day 2 ➤ Delhi - Jaipur
We will head for a day long tour to the capital city of India, Delhi. Delhi is filled with historic monuments that epitomize the city’s ancient glory and grandeur. Some of the major sightseeing includes Raj Ghat, Jama Masjid, Red Fort, Qutub Minar, Chandni Chowk, India Gate, Laxmi Narayan Temple and Humayun’s Tomb. After visiting Delhi, we drive for the Pink City, Jaipur. We will spend the night at the hotel in Jaipur.
Day 3 ➤ Jaipur
In Jaipur, we visit the amber fort by taking an elephant ride. It is just a few minutes’ drive from the capital city. Next, we tour to the City Palace Museum, Jantar Mantar and Hawa Mahal where we get the opportunity to explore the art and architecture of Jaipur. We return back to the hotel at night.
Day 4 ➤ Jaipur - Fatehpur Sikri - Agra
On the way to Agra, we visit Fatehpursikri, the city ruled by Mughal emperors for a long period of time. The majestic monuments of Fatehpursikri strike a unique combination of Indo-Saracenic architecture. The Tomb of Salim Chisti and Jama Masjid are the two other attractions of Fatehpursikri. It is a real pleasure visiting the historical place. We head to our next destination, Agra where we settle at the hotel for an overnight stay.
Day 5 ➤ Agra
The best creation for which the Agra city is known for is the Taj Mahal. The epitome of love and romance, the Taj Mahal, is an architectural excellence from the bygone era. The structure was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the fond memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal. Do not forget to visit the Agra fort, which is a World Heritage Site declared by UNESCO. You can shop from souvenirs to mementos at the local markets of Agra. We will spend the night at the hotel.
Day 6 ➤ Agra - Jhansi - Orchha - Khajuraho
After a delicious morning breakfast, we head for Jhansi. We will take a train journey to reach Jhansi, from where we proceed to Khajuraho. On the way, we come across Orchha, a place dotted with magnificent monuments and palaces carved with fine ancient architectures. After reaching Khajuraho, we take the day off and rest at the hotel room.
Day 7 ➤ Khajuraho - Delhi
Khajuraho is considered to be one of the finest historical sites of India. Housing a number of temples of Hindu and Jain community, the place is a UNESCO declared World Heritage Site. The structure of the temples is complex and mesmerizing. It makes you realize how rich and colorful was our ancient art and architecture. After the sightseeing at Khajuraho, we will take a flight to Delhi and spend the night at the hotel in Delhi.
Day 8 ➤ Delhi
Next day, our representative will drop you at the Delhi airport for your flight back home or we will arrange a train journey for you.
