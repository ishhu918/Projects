THE GLORY OF RAJASTHAN

  1. Home
  2. THE GLORY OF RAJASTHAN

Tour Description:

Rajasthan is a place known for its war stories, love tales and secrets about its desert. Our 8 days tour of Rajasthan will make you witness Royalty in the soil of this part of India. Without any second thought we can name our tour package as ‘The Glory of Rajasthan’.

We make you visit the busy streets of pink city, watch sunsets of Jaisalmer, relive your taste buds in Bikaner and enjoy the lakes and landscapes of Udaipur. We guarantee that exploring Rajasthan will be a life time experience with us.

Duration

8 Nights / 9 Days

Destination Covered

Delhi ➤ Jaipur ➤ Bikaner ➤ Jaisalmer ➤ Jodhpur ➤ Udaipur

Day 1 ➤ Delhi
Our representative will assist you from Delhi Airport. He will drop you at your hotel. The day is reserved for resting before you start your splendid journey in incredible India.
Day 2 ➤ Delhi-Jaipur
After having Breakfast, we will head for our first city tour to Jaipur. Jaipur is famous for its Palaces and Museums. Palaces were once home to Royal families but later turned into heritage hotels. Jantar Mantar and Hawa Mahal will be covered before returning back to our hotel.
Day 3 ➤ Jaipur
We will visit Ajmer fort, once the capital of princely state of Jaipur. The fort reflects royalty through its structure. We will take an elephant tour of the fort and rest of the day will be reserved for local shopping. The night will be spent at Jaipur hotel.
Day 4 ➤ Jaipur– Bikaner
Next on our list is visit to Bikaner. We will visit Juna garh fort in Bikaner, which is a unique fort built over desert. Later we will visit Camel Breeding farms for as they say, deserts are not complete without Camels.
Day 5 ➤ Bikaner– Jaisalmer
After a complete night rest and mouth tempting breakfast at Bikaner, we will head to our next destination – Jaisalmer. Fort Palace Museum, temples, Gadisar Lake and Patwaonki Haveli are tourist attractions of Jaisalmer. We retire for the day and look forward to more of Jaisalmer in the coming morning.
Day 6 ➤ Jaisalmer
After having our breakfast, we will head to Jaisalmer fort. Jaisalmer city is known for its forts and havelis. In the evening we will take camel rides as the sun sets and will return to our hotel and prepare for an early start.
Day 7 ➤ Jaisalmer– Jodhpur
After having breakfast early in the morning in Jaisalmer, we will head to the Sun City – Udaipur. Mehrangarh fort is the prime attraction in Udaipur. Later we will visit the Royal Cenotaphs at Jaswant thada. These white marble structures were built 120 years ago by the then emperor Maharaja Jaswant Singh.
Day 8 ➤ Jodhpur– Udaipur
With our last destination of tour in Rajasthan, we reach Udaipur. We will visit the Jain temple of Ranakpur and take a peaceful boat ride at Lake Pichola. Udaipur is famous for its lakes and Landscapes.
Day 9 ➤ Udaipur
On the last day of your tour, you will visit City Palace, Jagdish Temple. You will also visit Sahelion Ki Bari, which was made as meeting point for women.
With this we sign off and wish you luck with exploring culture, custom, traditions and colours of our Incredible India.
